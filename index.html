Vendor Approval
This is where you review incoming vendor applications. Vendors who fill in the Market Index form will appear here as pending. You can click on a vendor name to view their full details and stall photos, then select which vendors to approve or remove. When approving, you can choose exactly which market dates each vendor is allowed to attend.

Approved Vendors
All vendors you have approved will appear here. You can track which markets each vendor is attending, view their total stall fee, and monitor payment status. Use the "Update Payment" button to mark a vendor as paid via PayFast, Yoco, or manual cash/EFT — or to flag them as outstanding. You can also filter by payment status to quickly see who still owes.

Market Instances
Create and manage your individual market events here. Each market instance has its own name, description, banner image, stall fee, and dates. Once you publish a market instance, it becomes visible on the Market Index for vendors to browse and apply to. You can edit or unpublish a market at any time, and drafts will not be shown to vendors until published.
